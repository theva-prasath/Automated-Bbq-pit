# Automated Barbeque Pit Based on Auto Function

## Description
An automated rotating barbeque pit designed and fabricated 
as a Final Year Project at Polytechnic Nilai (2022). The 
system uses a motorised rotating mechanism to automatically 
rotate food during grilling, eliminating the need for manual 
turning and ensuring even cooking throughout the process.

## Achievement
🥇 Gold Medal — Final Year Project Exhibition 2022, 
Polytechnic Nilai

## Problem Statement
Traditional barbeque grilling requires constant manual 
attention to rotate food, which is time-consuming and 
inconsistent. Uneven rotation leads to overcooked or 
undercooked food. This automated system solves the problem 
by providing consistent, motor-driven rotation without 
human intervention.

## Features
- Automated motorised rotation using a 12V DC motor
- Drag racing chain mechanism for smooth power transfer
- Adjustable legs for height and angle flexibility
- Stainless steel grill for food-safe, heat-resistant cooking
- Mild steel structural body for durability and strength
- Compact and portable design

## Materials Used
| Component | Material | Reason |

| Structural Body | Mild Steel | High strength, cost-effective |
| Grill Surface | Stainless Steel | Food-safe, corrosion resistant |

## Technical Specifications
- **Motor:** 12V DC Motor
- **Drive System:** Drag racing chain and sprocket
- **Power Supply:** 12V DC
- **Frame Material:** Mild Steel
- **Grill Material:** Stainless Steel

## Material Analysis
A comparative study was conducted between **aluminium 
plate** and **mild steel** for the structural body:

| Property | Aluminium Plate | Mild Steel |
|----------|----------------|------------|
| Strength | Moderate | High |
| Weight | Light | Heavier |
| Cost | Higher | Lower |
| Heat Resistance | Moderate | High |
| **Selected** | ❌ | ✅ |

Mild steel was selected due to its superior strength, 
heat resistance, and cost-effectiveness for this 
application.

## How It Works
1. Power is supplied to the 12V DC motor
2. Motor drives the drag racing chain mechanism
3. Chain rotates the grill at a consistent speed
4. Food is grilled evenly without manual intervention
5. Adjustable legs allow positioning on uneven surfaces

## Cooking Trial Results
Cooking trials were conducted to validate the system 
performance. The automated rotation produced evenly 
cooked results compared to manual grilling, confirming 
the effectiveness of the motorised mechanism.
